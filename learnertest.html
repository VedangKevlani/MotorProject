Here is how to get your Learner's Permit in 6 easy steps:

Step1: Download and complete the H3A form by clicking on the button below:

Alternatively, you can visit the website to download the form: Provisional Driver's Licence

Step2:Download and complete the medical examination form by clicking on the button below:

Alternatively, you can visit the website to download the form: Provisional Drivers' Medical Form

Step3: Have 2 identical photographs taken no later than six months within applying for learners, certified by a Justice of the Peace and the back

Step4: Download and complete the proof of address form. Forms must be completed by a Justice of the Peace.

Alternatively, you can visit the website to download the form: Learner's Proof of Address Form

Step5: Visit the nearest examination centre/Island Traffic Authority office and complete the learner's test

Step6: Take your birth certificate or other government ID with date of birth, all completed documents and the sealed envelope with your test score to the Tax Office, pay $1800 JMD
